Why Agora Spaces

What Sets Us Apart

OUR EDGE: THE VISUAL PRESTIGE

Perched on the 16th floor of Max Towers, Agora Spaces offers panoramic dual-city views and 360° skyline visuals — a visual luxury truly unmatched in Delhi NCR. With a scenic backdrop overlooking DND, Yamuna, and the Delhi-Noida skyline, it's perfect for storytelling that captures the spirit of NCR and instantly elevates the aesthetic value of your content. Agora Spaces enjoys unmatched accessibility, ambience, and prestige — an environment that inspires creativity and confidence for every client, collaborator, and guest who walks through the door.

Our Production Suite

Everything you need to create, in one place

AUDIO & VIDEO

We're equipped with the best-in-class professional camera systems, featuring a 3-camera cinematic setup with multi-angle lens choices for both indoor and outdoor shoots. Studio-grade microphones paired with boom arms and shock mounts ensure every word is captured with broadcast-level clarity.

LIGHTING & VISUALS

Our full 5-point professional lighting system — covering key, fill, back, and ambience — is designed to create perfect tone and depth for every frame. A softbox and spotlight mix combined with adjustable color and mood lighting gives creators complete creative flexibility in every session.

BACKDROPS & PROPS

Our space features curated backdrop moods and premium furniture, décor, and props designed to create a polished on-camera presence. With quick backdrop switching, creators can achieve multiple distinct looks and styles within a single session — maximising output and variety.

WORKFLOW & TOOLS

A live monitoring station ensures real-time control throughout every shoot. Our on-set teleprompter system and optimised workflow tools deliver consistent, high-quality output every time. We are also fully equipped for live switching and real-time live streaming, enabling seamless multi-angle broadcasts directly from the studio.
